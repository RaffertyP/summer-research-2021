Release of monthly distance travelled and average efficiency data from user submitted data to Flip the Fleet up to and including September 2021.

Data dictionary:

vehicle: An anonymous Vehicle ID
model: The model of car
region: The region of New Zealand where the car is primarily located
season: The season of the month the data relates to
month: The month the data relates to
year: The year the data relates to
distance: The total distance travelled in that month (km)
efficiency: The efficiency of travel (km/kWh)
kwh: The estimated kWh used for that month (kWh) based upon the distance travelled and efficiency achieved. The actual kWh used will be higher due to charging losses. 

For any queries please contact wecan@flipthefleet.org
